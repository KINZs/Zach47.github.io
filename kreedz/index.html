About Me

Hello! This is my Kreedz website that I've worked on as a personal project. It was created as a way of improving my skills with HTML, CSS, and Javascript. With the help of Klyve and Chuckles, the website uses jQuery, json, and bootstrap.

I started playing Kreedz in August, 2014 on the 102 tick Tango World Wide server. Eventually I moved to 128 tick and played on House of Climb and KZ-Climb. Eventually, KZ-Climb migrated their servers with House of Climb, and I played on House of Climb as my main server. Now, I tend to play on my own private server which I use for streaming.

If you'd like to contact me, you can email me below or on my contact page. Thank you.
