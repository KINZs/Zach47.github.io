Sachburger

Sachburger is one of the few top KZers whose first game in the series was Counter Strike: Global Offensive. He started in late 2013 with his first server being House of Climb 1. His first map was kz_crash, where he was told where to go by SyZAxBEAST. As of now, his most commonly played map is kz_adv_cursedjourney, for which he has the pro and TP record for. Sachburger is currently the lead of the map testing team and a global admin.

If you'd like to know more about Sachburger's records, you can see them on his KZStats page.
