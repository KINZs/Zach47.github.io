Surfs

We are not going to cover in-depth surfing in relation to surf maps, but we will cover how to utilize surfs in KZ maps.

Basic surfing: Approach a surf ramp by jumping and trying to ‘land’ as far up the ramp as you can without losing speed. To ride a surf ramp sloped on a right wall, use ONLY your D key, and hold your crosshair at a parallel angle akin towards the direction that the surf ramp is going. To ride a surf ramp sloped on a left wall, just do the same with your A key. It’s quite easy once you get used to it, the harder part is approaching a ramp correctly, and strafing out of it correctly.

When approaching a ramp, you may sometimes want to strafe the opposite way you ride it in order to go down a portion of the ramp, ensuring that you gain speed to complete the surf ramp faster. When strafing out of surf ramps, you want to ride the surf ramp up with a smooth motion as high as possible without losing velocity, and then “strafe out” of it utilizing air strafing.
